About Application (short):
    Empowering businesses by providing a free web application tool to create and manage
    giftcards.

About Application (long):
    Giftcards provide an incentive for customers to come in once again and also incentivize
    new customers to come in.
    Many small businesses don't have access to giftcard management software unlike larger 
    corporations which can afford to develop or buy such software on their own.
    This free Gift Card Management Application hopes to level that playing
    field and provide small businesses with easy to use and free gift card management software.
    The software can generate codes, attach values to codes, edit the values and lookup codes.
    There are more features to come!
